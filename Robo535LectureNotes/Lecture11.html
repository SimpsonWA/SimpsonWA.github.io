Non-parametric Density estimation

  • The model of the distribution utilizes all examples in \(\mathcal{D}\)
  • As if all examples were parameters of the distribution
  • Example: Kernel density estimation, nearest neighbor density estimation

    Parameter Estimation

Maximum Likelihood Estimate (MLE):

  • Want to maximize $$ p(\mathcal{D}|\theta,\zeta) $$
  • Yields: 1 set of parameters \(\theta_{MLE}\)

  • Target distribution is approximated by: $$ \hat{p}(X) = p(X|\theta_{MLE}) $$

    Maximum a Posterior (MAP) estimation:

  • maximize : $$ p(\theta,\zeta|\mathcal{D}) $$

  • Yields 1 set of parameters \(\theta_{MAP}\)
  • Target distribution approximated by: $$ \hat{p}(X) = p(X|\theta_{MAP}) $$
